Submission Guidelines

General Requirements

  • Provide clear descriptions and documentation for your submissions
  • Include high-quality thumbnails or preview images
  • Ensure your submission doesn't contain malware or illegal content
  • Review the Team Salvato IP Guidelines before submitting

Mod Submissions

  • Include a direct download link (Google Drive, MEGA, etc.)
  • Specify the mod type (full mod, demo, etc.)
  • Mention any content warnings or age restrictions
  • Provide installation instructions if applicable

Asset Submissions

  • Specify usage restrictions or credits required
  • Include file format
  • Mention if the asset is original or derivative
  • For music, include track length, genre, and format details

Template Addon Submissions

  • Provide a brief description on what it does or is
  • Include setup/installation instructions
  • Specify compatibility with template versions
  • Mention any dependencies or requirements

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take for my submission to be reviewed?

Please have patience. Our team reviews submissions as quickly as possible, but we're a small team with other responsibilities. Submissions are typically processed in batches rather than individually.

Can I update my submission after it's been published?

Yes! You can submit updated versions through the same forms. Just make sure to mention that it's an update to an existing submission and provide details about what has changed.

Are there any restrictions on what I can submit?

We accept most submissions as long as they follow Team Salvato's IP Guidelines and don't contain illegal content. We archive EVERYTHING with very few exceptions.
